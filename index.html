Sovereignty Explorer

Assess and compare the digital sovereignty of technology suppliers

Prioritise by criteria Select up to 3 — suppliers are ranked from most to least relevant

Selecting a criterion ranks suppliers from the most to the least relevant on that criterion, based on their questionnaire answers — the sum of the 0–2 answer levels for its questions.

Selecting several criteria (up to 3) ranks suppliers by the average of their results across all of them.

Greyed-out criteria are not available for the current service-type filter. Suppliers with the exact same result are marked as tied, shown with a matching colour — never with a number.
